## ✨ New Features

### Schedule Notifications for Providers

Appointment reminders can now be sent to the **provider responsible for the appointment** — in addition to the client, or exclusively to them. Each notification template now includes a **Recipient** field with three options: client only, provider only, or both.

#### What is it for?

- **Remind the provider** of upcoming appointments without relying on manual processes.
- **Reduce no-shows and delays** from staff by automating WhatsApp or email alerts.
- **Full flexibility**: set up separate templates for the client and the provider with different messages and send times.

#### How does it work?

When creating or editing a schedule notification template, choose the recipient:

| Option | Behavior |
|--------|----------|
| **Client** | Sends only to the appointment's client (previous behavior) |
| **Provider** | Sends only to the provider linked to the appointment |
| **Both** | Sends to both client and provider in the same run |

#### Automatic contact synchronization

The system automatically links a notification customer record to each organization member based on their profile data (WhatsApp and email). On every reminder dispatch, the system:

1. Checks whether the member already has a linked notification record.
2. **If not**: creates one automatically from the profile's name, WhatsApp, and email.
3. **If the WhatsApp number has changed**: updates the existing contact to the current number, ensuring the provider always receives the message at the right number.
4. **If a new contact type (email) was added to the profile**: creates the complementary contact in the record.

No manual action is required from the administrator — the system keeps contact data always in sync.

## 🎯 Benefits

- ✅ Providers receive automatic appointment reminders via WhatsApp or email
- ✅ Per-template granular setup — different messages and times for client and provider
- ✅ Notification record created automatically on the first run, no manual configuration needed
- ✅ Contact automatically synced whenever the provider changes their number
- ✅ No impact on existing flows — templates without a defined recipient continue working for the client as before
