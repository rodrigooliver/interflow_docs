# v2026.6.2

Release date: **June 09, 2026**

---

## New Features

### Automatic Customer Deletion by Usage Limit

You can now configure automatic customer deletion when usage reaches your plan limit — working the same way as automatic storage cleanup. Deletion is processed asynchronously by the existing deletion queue, with no impact on system usage.

#### What is it for?

- Automatically keep your customer base within the plan limit, without manual deletion
- Remove the oldest/inactive customers first, preserving recent contacts
- Free up limit space for registering new customers

#### How to use?

1. Go to **Settings > Usage Limits**
2. Click the **Customers** card — it now opens a details panel (just like Storage)
3. Enable **Automatic Deletion** and adjust the settings:
   - **Start deletion when usage reaches**: percentage of the limit that triggers deletion (default 100%)
   - **Target usage after deletion**: target percentage after cleanup (default 90%)
   - **Deletion order**: by **last contact** (default) or **registration date**, always oldest first
   - **Delete linked conversations**: when disabled, customers with conversations are skipped (enabled by default)
   - **Run time** and **maximum customers per run**

### "Run Now" Button for Automatic Cleanups

The **Customers** and **Storage** panels now have a **Run now** button that triggers the cleanup immediately, without waiting for the scheduled time.

#### What is it for?

- Apply the cleanup right away, just after configuring it
- Track progress: while the run is in progress, the button shows **"In progress..."** and stays locked, preventing duplicate triggers
- Usage counters (including the limit alert in the sidebar) are automatically refreshed shortly after triggering

---

## Changes

### Customers with POS Orders Can Now Be Deleted

Previously, customers with POS orders could not be deleted (neither manually nor in bulk). Deletion is now allowed: orders are **preserved in the sales history**, only unlinked from the customer. Reports and financial records are not affected.

---

## Benefits

- ✅ Customer base always within the plan limit, with no manual work
- ✅ Smart deletion criteria: removes those without contact the longest first
- ✅ Immediate manual run with progress indicator — no repeated clicks
- ✅ Sales history (POS) preserved even after customer deletion
- ✅ Usage counters and sidebar alerts refreshed automatically
