## New Features

### Lead Timeline (Customer Journey)

New **Timeline** tab in the customer panel aggregates all important lead activity in chronological order: external contacts, CRM stage changes, conversations, notes, tasks and appointments.

#### What is it for?

- Managers track the full customer journey in one place
- Agents view history of their own actions during service
- Easier performance evaluation and sales process auditing

#### How to use?

1. Open any customer (list, CRM funnel or chat)
2. Go to the **Timeline** tab in the side panel
3. Scroll to load older events; expand feedback and note details

### Automatic external contact logging (personal WhatsApp)

When using the **Quick WhatsApp Button** in a conversation, the system automatically records that the agent contacted the customer outside Interflow.

#### What is it for?

- Document when service happens via personal WhatsApp
- Ensure traceability even outside connected channels

#### How to use?

1. In Settings > Organization, enable the **Quick WhatsApp Button**
2. In the customer chat, click the green **WhatsApp** button
3. The contact is logged in the timeline before opening `wa.me`

### Mandatory feedback after external contact

After external contact, the agent must fill in **feedback** with a summary and conversation details before continuing service in Interflow.

#### What is it for?

- Ensure every external conversation is documented
- Prevent service steps from going unrecorded

#### How to use?

1. After clicking the WhatsApp button, complete the **External contact feedback** modal
2. Enter a **summary** and describe the **conversation** (minimum 20 characters)
3. The message input stays blocked until feedback is submitted

#### Configuration

In **Settings > Organization > WhatsApp Button**, the **Require feedback after contact** toggle controls whether blocking is active (default: on).

## Changes

### Enriched timeline events

The backend now automatically records in the timeline:

- CRM funnel stage changes
- Conversation closures
- Chat assignment and transfer

### Timeline permissions

- **Managers** (`manager`, `admin`, `owner`) see the full timeline from all agents
- **Sales agents** (`sales`, `agent`) see events filtered to their own actions when applicable
