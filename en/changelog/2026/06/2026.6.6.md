## New Features

### "Include collaborating" filter in the conversations menu

New collaboration filter option lets you **expand** the conversation list with chats where you participate as a collaborator, without restricting results to those chats only.

#### What is it for?

- Combine existing filters (team, status, tags, etc.) and **also** see conversations where you collaborate
- Track multiple conversations more easily without switching tabs
- Useful on the **Team** tab or when filtering by assigned agent

#### How to use?

1. Open **Conversations** and click the filters icon
2. In the **Collaborating** section, select **Include collaborating**
3. Apply other filters as usual (status, team, agent, etc.)

Collaborating conversations appear even when they fail **assignment** filters (assigned agent or "exclude my assignments"). All other criteria still apply.

#### Quick filters

The same option is available in **quick filter configuration** (gear icon in the tab menu), so you can save custom combinations with "Include collaborating".

## Changes

### Collaboration filter modes

| Option | Behavior |
|--------|----------|
| Don't filter | No collaboration filter |
| **Include collaborating** | Normal results **+** collaborating conversations |
| Collaborating only | Only conversations where you collaborate |
| Not collaborating only | Excludes collaborating conversations |
