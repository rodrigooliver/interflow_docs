## New Features

### Setting to pause AI on external replies

New per-channel option lets you choose whether the flow/AI should be paused automatically when an agent replies **outside Interflow** (via the channel's native app).

#### What is it for?

- Give teams control over when automation should be interrupted
- Keep AI active even with manual external replies when desired
- Avoid unwanted pauses in operations that combine automation and phone-based support

#### How to use?

1. Open **Channels** and edit the desired channel (WhatsApp WAHA, WhatsApp Official, Instagram, or Facebook)
2. In **Settings**, find **Pause AI when replying outside Interflow**
3. **Enabled (default):** external replies pause the active flow/AI — previous behavior
4. **Disabled:** automation continues even when the agent replies via the channel app

#### Supported channels

| Channel | Where to configure |
|---------|-------------------|
| WhatsApp WAHA | Channel settings |
| WhatsApp Official | Official channel settings (phone echoes via auxiliary channel) |
| Instagram | Channel settings |
| Facebook Messenger | Channel settings |

---

## Changes

### Default behavior preserved

Existing channels without the setting keep automatic pause enabled (`true` by default), ensuring backward compatibility.

Messages sent **from the Interflow platform** never trigger this pause — only replies detected as external.

---

## Benefits

- ✅ More flexibility for teams that prefer to keep AI active
- ✅ Independent configuration per channel
- ✅ Previous behavior preserved as default
- ✅ Distinct from the organization setting "Pause AI when attending" (inside Interflow)
