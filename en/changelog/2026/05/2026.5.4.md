## ✨ New Features

### Bulk Actions in the Chat List

You can now perform actions on multiple chats at once — mark as read or mark as resolved — directly from the chat list, using the existing advanced filters.

#### What is it for?

- Clear queues quickly after bulk campaigns or volume spikes
- Mark all filtered chats as read (by tag, channel, agent, status, etc.) in one click
- Bulk-resolve **pending** conversations without opening each chat individually
- Reduce repetitive manual work for teams handling high conversation volume

#### How to use?

1. Open **Chats** and apply the desired filters (status, tag, channel, agent, team, unread only, etc.)
2. Click the **bulk selection** icon (☑) at the top of the chat list
3. Select chats individually with checkboxes **or** use **Select all filtered** to include every matching page
4. In the bottom bar, choose:
   - **Mark as read** — clears unread messages on eligible chats
   - **Mark as resolved** — closes only chats with **pending** status; **in progress** chats are skipped
5. Confirm the action when prompted (more than 10 chats)

> 💡 The operation runs as a single batch on the server, staying fast even with hundreds of conversations. Already-read chats or chats not in `pending` are counted as skipped in the summary.
