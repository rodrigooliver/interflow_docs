## ✨ New Features

### Closing WhatsApp group conversations

WhatsApp groups now use the same closing flow already available for individual conversations. You can mark a group as closed, remove it from the unread list, and keep only conversations that truly need attention in the spotlight.

#### What is it for?

- Organize the service queue by removing groups that no longer need follow-up
- Prevent groups from staying unread indefinitely
- Record the closure reason and summary, just like individual conversations
- Reopen the group when follow-up needs to resume

#### How to use?

1. Open the **WhatsApp group** in the conversation list
2. If the group is **pending**, click **Attend** to start follow-up
3. With the conversation **in progress**, click the green **Resolve** button (✓) at the top of the chat
   - On mobile: **⋮** menu → **Resolve**
4. In the modal, select the **closure type**, fill in the **summary**, and confirm
5. The group will be **closed** and removed from the **unread** list

#### Who can close?

- The **assigned agent** for the group
- Members with permission to **edit group information** (admins, moderators, or members authorized in group settings)
- For groups with no assigned owner: **organization admins and owners**

#### After closing

- The conversation shows *"This conversation has been closed"*, with date and time
- The summary remains visible in the conversation history
- To resume, use the **Reopen Chat** button
