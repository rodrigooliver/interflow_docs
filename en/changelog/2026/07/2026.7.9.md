## ✨ New Features

### Tool: Update Customer Contact

New ready-made AI Agent action to **save or update the customer’s phone, WhatsApp, or email** during the conversation.

#### What is it for?

- Collect a second phone or WhatsApp without typing into the record
- Update the email shared in chat (also on the customer’s main email field)
- Control which contact types the AI may write (phone, WhatsApp, and/or email)

#### How to use?

1. Open the **AI Agent** (Prompt) and go to **Actions / Tools**
2. Add the ready-made action **Update Customer Contact**
3. In the configuration, choose the allowed types (phone, WhatsApp, email)
4. Save and publish the prompt

> Full guide: [AI Tools — Update Customer Contact](/en/guide/ai-agents/tools#update-customer-contact)

---

### Tool: Update Customer Address

New ready-made AI Agent action to **save or update the customer address** during the conversation — from text (street, number, city, state, ZIP) or from a **GPS location** shared in chat.

#### What is it for?

- Collect delivery or billing address without a human agent typing into the record
- Accept a location pin (WhatsApp/WAHA) and fill the address automatically
- Keep the customer’s default address up to date from chat

#### How to use?

1. Open the **AI Agent** (Prompt) and go to **Actions / Tools**
2. Add the ready-made action **Update Customer Address**
3. Save and publish the prompt
4. In the conversation, when the customer shares an address or location, the AI calls the tool and writes it to the record

> Full guide: [AI Tools — Update Customer Address](/en/guide/ai-agents/tools#update-customer-address)

### Addresses on the customer record (CRM)

Customer edit now includes an **Addresses** section: list, create, edit, and delete addresses (including GPS coordinates and a map link).

#### What is it for?

- Manage delivery/billing addresses directly in the CRM
- See whether an address has GPS saved and open it in Google Maps
- Keep the manual record aligned with what the AI writes via tools

#### How to use?

1. Open a customer under **Customers** (or from chat)
2. In edit, scroll to the **Addresses** section
3. Click **Add** or edit an existing address
4. Fill street, number, city, ZIP, and optionally latitude/longitude

> Full guide: [CRM — Addresses](/en/guide/crm/customers#addresses)

## 🔄 Improvements

### Address in customer context (first message)

The **Customer info** block sent to the AI at the start of the conversation now includes the customer’s **saved addresses** (type, label, default, address lines, city, state, ZIP, country, and delivery instructions), in addition to name, contacts, tags, and other existing fields.

#### What is it for?

- The AI already knows the saved address without asking again
- Confirmations and updates are more accurate (e.g. “same delivery address?”)

---

### GPS coordinates and reverse geocoding

The address tool accepts **latitude/longitude** or coordinate text in the location-message format. When only GPS arrives, the system tries to complete street, city, and related fields via reverse geocoding (Google Maps if a key is set; otherwise Nominatim).

#### What is it for?

- The customer sends a pin on WhatsApp and the record gets filled in
- Fewer “what’s the street?” questions when location already arrived in chat
