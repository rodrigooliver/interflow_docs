# v2026.7.8

Release date: **July 14, 2026**

## ✨ New Features

### Audio in internal chats

You can now **record and send audio** directly in internal chats (direct messages and groups), using the same microphone control as WhatsApp channels.

#### What is it for?

- Communicate with the team faster and more practically
- Share instructions or context without typing long messages
- Keep internal support aligned with the team's pace

#### How to use?

1. Open a **direct chat** or **internal group** (Internal Chats page or floating widget)
2. In the message field, tap the **microphone** icon
3. Record the audio, confirm, and send

> Full guide: [Chat Interface — Internal Chat](/en/guide/chat/interface#internal-chat-communication-between-agents)

---

### Mark as read / unread in internal chats

On the **Internal Chats** list, each conversation now has a `⋯` menu to **mark as read** or **mark as unread**, matching the attendance list pattern. Opening an internal chat also marks pending messages as read automatically.

#### What is it for?

- Clear unread badges without having to reply
- Highlight conversations that still need attention
- Avoid confusion about what the team has already seen

#### How to use?

1. In **Internal Chats**, open the `⋯` menu next to the conversation
2. Choose **Mark as read** or **Mark as unread**
3. Or simply open the chat — the pending badge is cleared when you view it

---

### “Seen” status in internal direct chats

In **direct chats (DM)** between agents, opening the conversation marks received messages as **read/seen** for the sender (blue ticks), just like external channels.

#### What is it for?

- Know whether a teammate has already seen the message
- Reduce unnecessary follow-ups within the team
- Bring WhatsApp-like read clarity to internal chat

## 🐛 Bug Fixes

### “Mark as unread” undone while the chat is open

Fixed a behavior where marking an internal conversation as **unread** was immediately reverted to read because the chat was open. Auto mark-as-read now runs **only when opening** the conversation.

### Organization settings blocked for agents

Agents without access to the **Settings** module received a `Forbidden` error when loading preferences used in chat (signature, hide contact info, quick WhatsApp button, etc.), causing repeated requests on every message or update. **GET** for these settings is now allowed for any authenticated organization member; **editing** remains restricted to the Settings module.
