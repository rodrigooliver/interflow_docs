## ✨ New Features

### HR / Time clock module

New **HR / Time clock** module to record the workday with clock-in, break, return, and clock-out — with photo, GPS, and an audit trail.

#### What is it for?

- Punch time from the browser (desktop or mobile)
- Keep photo and location evidence on every punch
- Give employees their own profile and admins a team-wide view

#### How to use it?

1. A superadmin enables **HR / Time clock** under **Admin → Organizations → Modules**
2. Open **HR / Time clock → Punch**
3. Allow camera (if required) and location, then punch

> Full guide: [HR / Time clock](/en/guide/hr/)

---

### Workplaces and geofence

Register offices or sites with a radius (geofence). You can require presence on site, allow home office per person, or inherit the organization rule.

#### What is it for?

- Block punches outside the allowed location
- Allow home office only for those who need it
- Keep clear per-employee rules

#### How to use it?

1. Under **HR / Time clock → Workplaces**, add coordinates and radius
2. Under **Employees**, set each person’s geofence mode (inherit, required, home office, or custom locations)

> Full guide: [HR / Time clock](/en/guide/hr/)

---

### Hour bank and managerial payroll (with hours per day)

Hour-bank balance with movements, plus a payroll preview with regular hours, overtime, and absences — now with a **daily breakdown** (clock-in, clock-out, worked, expected, OT, and absence) and CSV export for the period or a **daily CSV**.

#### What is it for?

- Track hour credits and debits
- See **when** each employee worked each day
- Close a period with a consolidated view and export for analysis

#### How to use it?

1. **Hour bank** — check the balance; admins can make manual adjustments
2. **Payroll** — pick the period, generate a preview, review **Hours per day** (filter by employee), and export period CSV or daily CSV

> Full guide: [HR / Time clock](/en/guide/hr/)

---

### Weekly schedule per employee

In the employee form, admins set **which weekdays** count and **how many hours** each day (e.g. Mon–Fri 8h, Saturday 4h). Payroll uses this to compute expected hours, overtime, and absences.

#### What is it for?

- Reflect variable schedules in payroll
- Avoid incorrect absences on days the person does not work

#### How to use it?

1. Under **Employees**, create or edit the person
2. In **Weekly schedule**, enable days and set hours
3. Save — the **Payroll** preview uses that schedule

> Full guide: [HR / Time clock](/en/guide/hr/)

---

### HR calendar (holidays and non-working days)

**Calendar** page to register organization holidays and non-working days (optional yearly recurrence). Those days **zero expected hours** in payroll.

#### What is it for?

- Avoid charging absence on holidays or closed days
- Keep a single organization calendar

#### How to use it?

1. Open **HR / Time clock → Calendar**
2. Add date, title, type (holiday or no work), and yearly recurrence if needed

> Full guide: [HR / Time clock](/en/guide/hr/)

---

### Medical certificates and individual days off

On the employee detail **Absences** tab: register a medical certificate or individual day off (date range, note/where it was presented, and **attachment**). These also zero expected hours in payroll.

#### What is it for?

- Justify absences with a document
- Grant a one-off day off without changing the org calendar

#### How to use it?

1. Open the employee detail → **Absences**
2. Click **Register**, fill type, dates, note, and attach a file if needed
3. Save

> Full guide: [HR / Time clock](/en/guide/hr/)

---

### Push notifications on punch

Admins choose, per employee, who receives a OneSignal push when that person punches. The link opens the employee’s HR detail.

#### What is it for?

- Alert managers or HR in real time
- Speed up timekeeping review

#### How to use it?

1. Open **Employees** and the person’s detail
2. On the **Notifications** tab, select recipients and save

> Full guide: [HR / Time clock](/en/guide/hr/)

---

### My HR profile (employee)

Employee area at **/My HR profile**, separate from the admin view: punches, hour bank, and payroll **without** photos, IP, or notification settings.

#### What is it for?

- Review your own timekeeping privately
- Keep personal and management views clearly separated

#### How to use it?

1. In **HR / Time clock**, open **My HR profile**
2. Filter punches by date and check hour bank / payroll

> Full guide: [HR / Time clock](/en/guide/hr/)

---

### Punch details with on-demand photo (admin)

In the punches list, photos are **not** loaded in bulk (so the page opens faster). Admins use **Details** to open a modal: photo, IP, GPS, and device load there. The map remains available from coordinates.

#### What is it for?

- Audit who punched and from where
- Avoid slowness when listing many punches with photos

> Full guide: [HR / Time clock](/en/guide/hr/)

## 🔄 Improvements

### Punching without a webcam

If a photo is required and the computer has no camera (or permission is denied), the employee can **upload a photo** file. If the organization turns off photo requirement, punching works with GPS only (when required).

### Mirrored camera preview

The preview is mirrored (selfie-style) for easier framing. The saved photo keeps normal orientation.

### Loading states on HR screens

HR pages show a loading state while fetching data, avoiding a brief empty flash.

### Day status on payroll

In the **Hours per day** table, each row shows status (work, holiday, medical certificate, individual day off, no work), aligned with the calendar and absences.
