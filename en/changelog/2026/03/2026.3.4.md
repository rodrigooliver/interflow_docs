## ✨ New Features

### 📊 Message Volume Reports

New reports module available in the sidebar menu, with two complementary views to track the productivity of the support team.

#### What is it for?

- **View by agent** – See how many messages each team member sent in any period, with an evolution chart over time
- **View by team** – Compare message volume across the organization's teams
- **Flexible period selection** – Choose from today, this week, this month, this year, or a specific date/month/year
- **Filter by agent or team** – Click a summary card to focus the chart on a single agent or team
- **Built-in printing** – Generate a clean printable version with the period header and totals

#### How to use?

1. In the sidebar, click **Reports** (bar chart icon)
2. Choose **Volume by Agent** or **Volume by Team**
3. Select the desired period in the selector at the top right
4. Click an agent/team card to filter the chart
5. Use the **Bar** / **Line** buttons to toggle the chart type
6. Click the **Print** button to generate the print version

::: info Permissions
Access to the Reports module is exclusive to **Owners**, **Administrators**, and **Managers**. Agents (agent and agent_limited) do not see this item in the menu and cannot access the pages directly.
:::
