## ✨ New Features

### 👥 WhatsApp Groups (WAHA) – Full experience

Set of features for **external groups** linked to **WhatsApp WAHA** channels, bringing the experience closer to apps like WhatsApp: add participants as customers, delete messages from other members, group picture, and participants list.

#### What is it for?

- **Add participant as customer** – From the message menu (⋯) of a participant, register them as a customer and start a private conversation
- **Delete messages** – Group admins can delete messages from other participants (as well as their own)
- **Group picture** – Shown automatically in the chat header
- **Participants list** – Modal with group participants (WAHA API) and “Add as customer” for those with a visible number

#### How to use?

1. **Add as customer (from message):** Open the group chat → find a message on the **left** (from a participant) → click the **menu (⋯)** → **“Add as customer”** → complete registration.
2. **Delete message:** Message menu (⋯) → **“Delete message”** (also available for others’ messages; on WhatsApp, only admins can delete third-party messages).
3. **Group picture:** Loaded automatically in the header when you open the chat.
4. **Participants:** In the group chat header, click **“Participants”** → view the list and use **“Add as customer”** for those with an available number.

::: info Privacy (Meta)
Some participants appear only with an identifier (@lid) due to Meta policies. In those cases, use **“Add as customer”** from the **message menu** when the participant sends a message in the group.
:::
