## New Features

### Multiple Chats Indicator

Visual indicator when a customer has multiple active conversations.

#### How does it work?

- 🔴 Badge appears next to customer name
- Shows number of parallel chats
- Clicking reveals the list of active conversations
- Quick navigation between chats

---

### Direct Page Navigation

Jump directly to a specific page in listings.

#### How to use?

1. At the bottom of any paginated list
2. Click on the page number field
3. Type the desired page number
4. Press Enter

#### Available in:
- 📋 Customer list
- 💬 Conversation list
- 📅 Appointments
- 📊 Reports

---

### WhatsApp Groups Support

Receive and respond to messages from WhatsApp groups.

#### How does it work?

- Groups appear in the conversation list
- Identify who sent in the group
- Respond directly to the group
- Full history preserved

#### Permissions:
- Admin can enable/disable per channel
- Agents can be restricted from groups

::: warning Attention
Currently works only with WAHA channel.
:::
