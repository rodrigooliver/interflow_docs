## New Features

### Agent Assignment and Removal by Administrator

Administrators and owners can now manage agent assignment in conversations without entering each conversation individually.

#### What you can do?

- ✅ Transfer conversation to another **team**
- ✅ Assign a **specific agent**
- ✅ Remove agent and leave conversation **pending** in queue
- ✅ Manage support directly from the list

#### How to access?

The **"Transfer Agent/Team"** option is available in:

1. **Conversation List** - Three dots menu (⋮)
2. **Customer History** - Actions menu
3. **Inside Conversation** - Menu in top right corner

#### Transfer options

| Option | What happens |
|--------|--------------|
| No agent | Conversation stays pending in team queue |
| Specific agent | Conversation transferred and marked as "In progress" |

#### Permissions

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| Owner | ✅ Yes |
| Admin | ✅ Yes |
| Member | ❌ No |

---

### Filters Blocked for Limited Users

Users with limited profile now have restricted access to system filters.

#### What changes?

- Filters are hidden or disabled for limited profiles
- Only users with adequate permissions can use filters
- Greater permission control and security
