### Message Search Within Conversations

You can now **search for messages within each individual conversation**, whether it's an active or closed conversation. Previously, you could only search messages across all conversations, making it difficult to quickly find specific information in a support session.

#### What is it for?

- Quickly locate **specific information** in long conversations
- Find **exact excerpts** from past conversations
- Save time searching for records in **message history**
- Works in both **ongoing** and **archived or closed** conversations

#### How to use?

1. **Open the conversation** you want to search
2. **Click the menu** (‚ò∞ icon) in the top right corner
