## New Features

### Light Mode on Android and iOS App

The mobile app now supports **Light Mode**! Previously, only dark mode was available. Now you can choose between **Light Mode** or **Dark Mode** according to your preference.

#### How to change?

1. Go to the **Sidebar** (left menu)
2. Switch between **Light** or **Dark** mode

---

### Message Search Within Conversations

You can now **search for messages within each individual conversation**, whether it's an active or closed conversation. Previously, you could only search messages across all conversations, making it difficult to quickly find specific information in a support session.

#### What is it for?

- Quickly locate **specific information** in long conversations
- Find **exact excerpts** from past conversations
- Save time searching for records in **message history**
- Works in both **ongoing** and **archived or closed** conversations

#### How to use?

1. **Open the conversation** you want to search
2. **Click the menu** (☰ icon) in the top right corner
3. **Select "Search in conversation"** 🔍
4. **Type what you're looking for** (minimum 2 characters)
5. **Navigate through results** using the ↑ ↓ arrows
6. **Press Enter** or click the result to go to the message

#### Keyboard Shortcuts

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| ↑ ↓ | Navigate between results |
| Enter | Go to selected message |
| Esc | Close search |

#### Features

- ✅ Search term appears **highlighted in yellow** in results
- ✅ Shows the message **sender** (Agent, Customer, or System)
- ✅ Displays the **date/time** of each message
- ✅ **Progress bar** when navigating to older messages
- ✅ Works on **mobile and desktop devices**
