### Quick WhatsApp Button

New button to open customer's WhatsApp directly from chat, with pre-configured message and automatic actions.

#### What is it for?

- Contact customer outside the system
- Send standard message quickly
- Automatically update information when clicking (field, funnel, tag)

#### How to configure?

1. Go to **‚öôÔ∏è Settings** in the sidebar
2. In the **"Support"** section, locate **"Quick WhatsApp Button"**

   - **Custom field** to update (optional)
   - **Funnel stage** to move to (optional)

#### How to use?

1. Open a WhatsApp customer chat
2. In the header, click the green WhatsApp button
3. New tab opens with message ready
4. Configured actions are executed automatically
