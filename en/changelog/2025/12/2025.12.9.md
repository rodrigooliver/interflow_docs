## New Features

### Quick WhatsApp Button

New button to open customer's WhatsApp directly from chat, with pre-configured message and automatic actions.

#### What is it for?

- Contact customer outside the system
- Send standard message quickly
- Automatically update information when clicking (field, funnel, tag)

#### How to configure?

1. Go to **⚙️ Settings** in the sidebar
2. In the **"Support"** section, locate **"Quick WhatsApp Button"**
3. Activate the toggle to enable
4. Configure in the modal:
   - **Default message** (use `{{customerName}}` for customer name)
   - **Custom field** to update (optional)
   - **Funnel stage** to move to (optional)
   - **Tag** to add (optional)
5. Click **"Save"**

#### How to use?

1. Open a WhatsApp customer chat
2. In the header, click the green WhatsApp button
3. New tab opens with message ready
4. Configured actions are executed automatically

::: warning Important
Button only appears in WhatsApp channel chats (WAHA, W-API, Official WhatsApp).
:::

### WAHA Message Sync

Button to fetch and sync WhatsApp historical messages that didn't enter automatically.

#### How to use?

1. Open a WAHA chat
2. Click on menu (⋮) → **"Sync Messages"**
3. Configure the period:
   - **Start Date** and **End Date**
   - Maximum limit: 3 days per sync
4. Click **"Sync"**

#### What happens?

- ✅ Fetches messages from selected period
- ✅ Syncs with original timestamp
- ⏭️ Ignores existing messages
- ✅ Updates correct status (sent, delivered, read)
