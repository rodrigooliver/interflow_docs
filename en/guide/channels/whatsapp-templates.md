# WhatsApp Templates (Official API)

Manage Meta templates to start conversations outside the 24h window, bulk sends, and new chats.

::: tip Access
**Channels →** WhatsApp Business (Official) → **Templates**
:::

Sync status from Meta. Only **APPROVED** templates can be used in chat and [bulk messages](/en/guide/chat/bulk-messages).

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| APPROVED | Ready |
| PENDING | Under review |
| REJECTED | Fix and resubmit |
