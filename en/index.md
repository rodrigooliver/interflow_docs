## 🚀 Quick Start

Follow these steps to start using Interflow:

<div class="tip custom-block" style="padding-top: 8px">

1. **Create your account** - Go to [app.interflow.chat/signup](https://app.interflow.chat/signup) and register
2. **Set up your organization** - Add your company information
3. **Connect a channel** - Integrate WhatsApp, Instagram, or another channel
4. **Invite your team** - Add members and define permissions
5. **Start serving** - Receive and manage conversations

</div>
